Map

Dive into the apocalyptic world of Project Zomboid with our interactive map, an essential tool for survivors looking to adapt and survive in this desolate setting. The game offers a vast and detailed recreation of a city infested with zombies, and understanding the terrain around you could be the key to your survival.

Here, you'll find a complete overview of major locations like shelters, stores, gas stations, and points of interest, along with details about the best escape routes and strategic spots to stay safe. Our map is constantly updated, considering the latest game changes and updates, ensuring that you always have the most accurate and useful information for your adventures in Project Zomboid.

Get ready, survive, and explore with confidence. The post-apocalyptic world of Project Zomboid can be unforgiving, but with the help of this map, you'll be one step ahead of the horde.
